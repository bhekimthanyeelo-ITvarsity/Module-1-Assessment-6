About Us

Africa’s largest discount online shop

Home Electronics is a promotionally driven discount retailer of predominantly general merchandise and non-perishable groceries for home, leisure and business use.

Home of Electronics is a subsidiary of the JSE listed, Massmart Holdings. Massmart is Africa’s third largest distributor of consumer goods, the

leading retailer of general merchandise, and home improvement equipment and supplies, and the leading wholesaler of basic

entertianment.

Home of Electronics online store was opened in South Africa, in 1970. It was born out of the belief by its founders that shopping had

become a tedious and boring pastime. They wanted to create a fun shopping environment and so conceptualized retailing as a Home of Electronics.

The signature shocking pink has still prevailed to this day.
